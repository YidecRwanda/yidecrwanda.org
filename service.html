Climate Change Education and Outreach

Empowering Young Quakers for Environmental Activism" YIDEC is dedicated to training young Quakers worldwide to become passionate environmental activists. Through our comprehensive program, we equip them with the knowledge and skills needed to foster a harmonious relationship with their environment and promote climate sustainability. Our focus is on instilling a mindset shift among young Quakers, empowering them to take proactive steps towards caring for our planet's future. Join us in our commitment to inspire and motivate youth worldwide to champion climate change sustainability

Afforestation

Deliberate process of planting trees in areas where there were no trees previously. Afforestation projects aim to increase the carbon sink capacity of ecosystems, restore degraded lands, enhance biodiversity, and provide various ecosystem services while combating climate change.

Climate Resilience Support.

Emphasizing on the provision of guidance, assistance, and support to young people who may be dealing with the psychological, social, or physical impacts of climate change, helping them to develop resilience and coping strategies in the face of these challenges.

Climate Resilience Peacebuilding.

Emphasizing on the dual focus on building resilience to climate change impacts while fostering peace and understanding among communities, including young people. It encompasses efforts to address conflicts or tensions arising from climate change-related challenges and promote cooperation, dialogue, and collaboration among diverse groups affected by environmental changes.

Our Partners Say!!!

Evangelical Friends Church of Rwanda, EEAR

Kigali, Rwanda

The Friends Church—also known as Religious Society of Friends (Quakers)—began in the middle of the 17th century in England. Since that time we have borne witness to the love of God in Jesus Christ through what we call "Testimonies." Evangelical Friends Church Rwanda, founded in 1987, continues that witness by raising up the following "values:Fellowship, Transparency, Stedwardship, Humility, Integrity, Pacifism, Cleanliness, Equality"

Transformational Leadership Center, TLC

Kigali, Rwanda

TThe Transformational Leadership Center is a Rwandan nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering communities around Rwanda to transform their livelihoods. By building conflict management and leadership skills among community leaders and NGOs, we mobilize communities to work together for poverty reduction, promotion of education, and a peaceful future.

Quacker Earthcare Witness, QEW

North America

Quaker Earthcare Witness (QEW) is a network of Friends (Quakers) in North America working on Earthcare concerns. We work to inspire Spirit-led action toward ecological sustainability and environmental justice.

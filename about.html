About Us

Welcome to Yidec

Youth Impacts Development and Environmental Change! We're dedicated to educating and empowering youth to become activists for environmental and climate change sustainability. Through educational initiatives, we equip young people with the knowledge and skills to advocate for a better future for our planet.

Join us in our mission to amplify youth voices and combat the effects of climate change for the sake of a healthier and more sustainable world. Together, we can make a meaningful difference in preserving our planet for generations to come. Explore our website to learn more and get involved.
